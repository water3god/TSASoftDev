Environmental Issues

There are many environmental issues that have different effects on the world we live in. Learn about them here then go to the next page to find out how we can help.

  • Fossil Fuel Dependence

    Our world relies very heavily on fossil fuels that are detrimental to drill and release CO2 when burned. This CO2 is a greenhouse gas, trapping heat in our atmosphere.

  • Plastic pollution

    In 2015, we produced 419 million tons of plastic. About 14 million tons per year ends up in the ocean per year. Plastic breaks down to microplastics and these deplete the overall water quality. Plastic products also do not biodegrade, so they will be there forever unless we do something about it.

  • Climate Change

    Climate change has brought upon many negative impacts to our environment such as droughts and flooding. Enviroments such as the arctic are most vulnerable due to warming being twice as fast as the global average. Many environments home to a variety of species aresuffering from climate change. Invasive species may start to come thrive in affected environments due to the environment being more detail because of climate change. With the help of everyone supporting the reduction of emissions to zero caused by greenhouse gas emissions, warming will be limited and wil help combat climate change.

  • What you can do

    Teach others to be more aware of climate change. Find enviornmentally friendly alternatives to technologies you use. Instead of using a car, consider biking or walking. Save energy whenever you can. Turn of your lights and AC when you are not using them.

Environmental Issues

There are many environmental issues that have different effects on the world we live in. Learn about them here then go to the next page to find out how we can help.

  • Fossil Fuel Dependence

    With the usage of carbon taxes (tax on carbon emission released from burning fossil fuels), carbon emissions are being used less and limits the rise of global temperature.

  • Plastic Pollution

    With the help of all communites pitching in and helping collect plastic, the dire effects that plastic pollution has can be limited.

    • Climate Change

    With the help of everyone supporting the reduction of emissions caused by greenhouse gas emissions, warming will be limited and wil help combat climate change.

    Ocean Acidification

    Through limiting plastic pollution and global warming, ocean acidification can be prevented or at least slowed. The carbon tax and communities pitching in solutions also help to combat ocean acidification as they combat plastic pollution and global warming.

    Deforestation

    Through limiting deforestation can forests continue to thrive and avoid extinction. With the help of people willingly planting seeds for trees near forests going through deforestation to grow, certain parts of forests will be limited from being cut down while the other parts are permitted so the effects of forests being able to combat soil erosion will still be present while people can still cut down trees without impacting the environment more negatively.
